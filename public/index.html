About PlanetaryFi

PlanetaryFi is a living universe on the blockchain, where every holder becomes a planet on the cosmic map. The more tokens you hold, the larger and brighter your planet grows, orbiting around the central sun — the founder’s wallet.

But this cosmos is alive with more than just visuals: every five minutes, half of the developer fee is randomly distributed to one lucky holder. This mechanic turns the galaxy into a dynamic system of rewards, making every orbit an opportunity.

PlanetaryFi isn’t just a token — it’s a universe shaped by its community, where holding means growing, shining, and having a chance to receive unexpected cosmic drops.
